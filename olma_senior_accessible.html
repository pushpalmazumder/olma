Sample Guide — Step 1 of 6
📦
What's in Your Kit
Your kit contains:

● 1 × Lancet (finger-prick device)
● 1 × Blood collection tube (labelled)
● 1 × Alcohol wipe
● 1 × Adhesive plaster
● 1 × Biohazard bag
● Pre-paid return envelope
● This instruction card

Check everything is present before starting.
9:41●●● 🔋
Sample Guide — Step 2 of 6
🕑
When to Collect
Best time: First thing in the morning

For accurate results:

● Fast for 8–12 hours beforehand (water is fine)
● Do not exercise on the morning of collection
● Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before
● Stay well hydrated the day before

Do not collect if you are unwell — wait until you feel better.
9:41●●● 🔋
Sample Guide — Step 3 of 6
🧪
How to Collect
Step by step:

1. Wash your hands with warm water for 20 seconds

2. Warm your hand by rubbing together for 2 minutes

3. Clean the side of your ring finger with the alcohol wipe. Let it dry.

4. Hold the lancet firmly against the side of your fingertip and press the button

5. Gently squeeze your finger to produce drops of blood

6. Fill the tube to the fill line — this may take 10–15 drops

Never collect from the very tip of your finger.
9:41●●● 🔋
Sample Guide — Step 4 of 6
🏷
Seal and Label
After collecting:

1. Cap the collection tube firmly

2. Write your full name and date of birth on the label on the tube

3. Place the tube in the biohazard bag

4. Seal the biohazard bag

5. Place inside the return envelope with the instruction sheet

Do not refrigerate your sample.
9:41●●● 🔋
Sample Guide — Step 5 of 6
📩

Your kit includes a pre-paid return label — no postage needed.

● Drop it at any post office or post box
